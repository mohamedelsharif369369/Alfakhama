Oenos Room

Adjacent to the Kouzina is the Oenos or wine room, featuring large windows that look out on to Jackson Street and a dramatic wall of wine racks that spans floor to ceiling. The large, wood-framed, sliding glass doors that separate the Oenos Room from the main dining area allows guests to feel part of the restaurant while still being in a private space.

Comfortably accommodates up to 30 people seated or 40 people for a standing reception.

zoom_out_map

Hania Room

This unique private dining room is located downstairs and is completely separate from the main dining area. The Hania Room room has the feel of a wine cellar with a rectangular polished wood table, warm wood accents and rich, tapestry carpets.

Ideal for small business meetings and intimate family functions,
Accommodates up to 10 people.

zoom_out_map

Chef’s Table

The focal point of the Kouzina is the Chefs Table. A beautiful 20 foot hand-carved wooden table.

Comfortably accommodates up to 20 people.

zoom_out_map

ENTIRE Restaurant

The entire restaurant is available for cocktails, passed Hors doeuvres, or a sit down your special event.

Comfortably accommodates up to 120 seated, 200 for a standing reception.
